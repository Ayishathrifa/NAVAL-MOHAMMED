ABOUT ME

Welcome to my world of capturing moments through the lens! I'm Naval Mohammed, a seasoned photographer with a unique journey that intertwines the worlds of business and creativity. With a post-graduate degree in marketing, I currently thrive in the dynamic environment of one of the Big 4 firms. However, my passion for photography, which ignited back in 2013, has been the driving force behind my artistic endeavors. It all began when a close friend persuaded me to pick up a camera and explore the art of photography. Little did I know, that initial step would turn into a profound lifelong journey. From capturing the vibrant streets of bustling cities to the tranquil serenity of remote landscapes, photography quickly transformed from a hobby to a vocation that feeds my soul. Over the years, my lens has framed countless tales of beauty, wonder, and wanderlust. Specializing in landscape and travel photography, I've had the incredible privilege of witnessing the world through my camera. Whether it's the rugged cliffs of coastal vistas, the untouched wilderness of hidden forests, or the intricate architecture of ancient towns, each photograph I take encapsulates a moment in time that tells a unique story. My dedication and artistic vision have been recognized on both national and international stages. I'm humbled to have received multiple awards that celebrate my commitment to my craft. These accolades not only fuel my passion but also inspire me to keep pushing the boundaries of my art. Photography, for me, is more than just capturing stills; it's about freezing emotions, weaving narratives, and inviting viewers to embark on visual journeys alongside me.
